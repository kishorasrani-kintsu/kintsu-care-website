Beyond the Passport

Kintsu Digital: the operating system underneath.

The Passport is the patient-facing layer of a broader platform — Kintsu Digital - Oncology and Kintsu Digital - Dialysis. It's what runs both of Kintsu's own centers, and it's built so that any independent oncology or dialysis center in India will be able to license it as software, whether or not Kintsu operates the center.
